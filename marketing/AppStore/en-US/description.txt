Find someone you just get along with.

Getalong is a calmer way to meet new people — through small signals, not another profile photo. Share one honest line, notice someone on your wavelength, and if it clicks, talk live.

How it works
• Share a small signal: one line, one mood, one moment.
• Notice someone whose words land for you.
• Send a live signal. They have a short window to respond.
• If you both feel it, a private chat begins.

Built for real conversation
• Words first, not photos. People come for what you say.
• Live signals are quick, mutual moments — not infinite swiping.
• Missed signals don't disappear; you can still respond later.
• Private one-to-one chats only. No group noise.

Designed to feel calm
• Light and dark mode from day one.
• No flashy gimmicks or pressure tactics.
• Block and report on every surface.

Getalong is for adults 18 and over.

Privacy
