# Getalong Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-26_

This document explains what information Getalong collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. It is a working draft and must be reviewed by counsel before publication. Plain English first, defined terms where they help.

## 1. Who we are

Getalong is operated by the Getalong team. Contact: **support@getalong.app**.

## 2. What we collect

When you create an account and use Getalong, we collect:

- **Account information.** Your sign-in identifier from Apple, Google, Facebook, or X. We do not see your password.
- **Profile information.** Your handle, display name, optional one-line signal, optional gender, optional "want to see" preference, optional region, optional language.
- **Signals you send.** Live signals, missed signals, and the timestamps and outcomes of each.
- **Chats.** Messages you send in private one-to-one chats. We don't read them in the normal course of operating the service.
- **One-time-view media.** Images, GIFs, and short videos you choose to send. View-once items are auto-deleted after they are opened.
- **Device info.** App version, OS version, device model, locale, push notification token if you opt in.
- **Diagnostics.** Crash reports and limited error logs.

We do **not** collect: your contacts, your photos library at large, your precise location, advertising identifiers, or biometric data.

## 3. How we use it

- To provide the service: account, signals, chats, media delivery.
- To enforce safety: blocking, reporting, banning abusive accounts.
- To send notifications you opt into.
- To diagnose crashes and improve the app.
- To meet legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data.

## 4. Third-party providers

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, storage, edge functions.
- **Apple Push Notification service (APNs)** — delivery of push notifications.
- **Apple, Google, Facebook, X** — sign-in providers (only the identifier we receive from them).

These providers are bound by their own terms; their use of data is governed by their privacy policies.

## 5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Access the data we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Delete your account and associated data.
- Object to or restrict certain processing.
- Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

You can delete your account from Profile → Delete account, or by emailing **support@getalong.app**.

## 6. Children

Getalong is not for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor has signed up, contact **support@getalong.app** and we will remove the account.

## 7. Retention

- Profile + signals + chat metadata: kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion (some logs retained up to 90 days for fraud and abuse investigation).
- Chat messages: deleted with the chat room or with your account.
- One-time-view media: deleted as soon as the recipient opens it, or within 30 days if unviewed.
- Diagnostics: kept up to 90 days, then aggregated or deleted.

## 8. Security

We use Apple platforms' standard transport security, server-side row-level access control, and signed URLs for private media. No system is perfect; you should keep your device locked and your sign-in account secure.

## 9. International transfers

Data is processed on the servers of our hosting provider, which may be located outside your country. Where required, we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

## 10. Changes

We will post material updates here and, where appropriate, notify you in-app.

## 11. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: **support@getalong.app**.
