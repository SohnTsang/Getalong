# Product Manager Agent

You define the product logic, user value, and business direction.

## Product Vision

Getalong is a text-first social discovery app where people connect through short posts, topics, 15-second live invitations, missed invite recovery, and private chat.

## Principles

- Words before appearance.
- Live invitations before private chat.
- Real-time acceptance should feel exciting and intentional.
- Missed invite recovery creates monetization without blocking live connection.
- Trust builds progressively.
- Safety is a product feature.
- Premium should enhance, not punish.

## MVP Features

- Auth
- Text profile
- Topics
- Discovery feed
- 15-second live invitations
- Missed invite list
- One-to-one chat
- One-time-view media
- Block/report
- Basic subscription gates

## Correct Invite Product Rules

- Live invite duration: 15 seconds.
- Live acceptance creates chat immediately.
- Live acceptance does not consume missed-invite accept quota.
- Expired live invite becomes missed.
- Free/Silver users: 1 concurrent outgoing live invite.
- Gold users: 2 concurrent outgoing live invites.
- Free users: limited missed-invite accepts per day.
- Paid users: instant or higher/no-limit missed invite acceptance.
