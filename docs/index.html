Safety & privacy

Designed for safer conversations.

View-once media can only be opened once in the app. For safety and abuse-prevention, media may be retained privately for up to 24 hours. Reported content may be kept longer for review.

We don’t use your data for advertising. We don’t sell your data. We don’t pretend a phone screen can stop a screenshot — we just build the rest of the system to be as careful as we can.

  • Report tools next to every conversation, message, and profile.
  • Block tools that take effect immediately on both sides.
  • No public media browsing — chat media stays in the chat.
  • Privacy-aware design: row-level access, signed URLs, no ads SDK.

FAQ

A few honest answers.

Is Getalong a dating app?

Getalong is a social discovery app for finding people you naturally get along with. What it becomes depends on the conversation.

Why only 10 Discovery cards?

Because Getalong is designed to feel calmer than endless scrolling. You see a small batch, then refresh when you want a new set.

How do Live Invites work?

A Live Invite gives someone a short moment to respond. If they accept, a chat starts.

What happens to view-once media?

It can only be opened once in the app. For safety, it may be retained privately for up to 24 hours, and reported content may be kept longer for review.

Can I report or block someone?

Yes. Reporting and blocking are available from chat and other relevant surfaces.

When does it launch?

Getalong is iOS first and coming soon. Email support@getalong.app if you’d like to be told when it’s ready.
